About Me

I grew up a child of the 80's in Morrinsville, New Zealand. I was always running around until I discovered the likes of Sonic and Mario. I have memories of Atari, 20c arcades at the fish 'n' chips shop and computers becoming a thing with Windows '95, DOS, and big floppy disks. I then moved and lived in Whangarei, New Zealand for about 12 years before moving here to Christchurch in 2019.

I also grew up around horses as my Dad was a Harness Racing Trainer with pacers and the odd trotter. But I have always been bit of a pop culture, gaming, computer, geek.

I still game from time to time depending on how busy I am. I'm currently enjoying some Baldur's Gate 3 and Monster Hunter Rise. I also enjoy going for walks or extended walks. During the pandemic I got into running which still comes and goes in phases depending on how busy I am.

I used to be into a bit of filming and video editing for youtube and purchased a Panasonic G7 camera, however that is now primarily used for photography from time to time. I have a hearing loss for which I wear hearing aids.
